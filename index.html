Evidence beyond the resume

BR41N.IO BCI Hackathon · 3rd place

Placed third overall in the BR41N.IO Brain-Computer Interface Designers Hackathon as part of the team Lobes of Confusion, contributing to a stroke rehabilitation data analysis challenge alongside Nicholas Ross, Jonathan Pham, Tharan Suresh, and Aaron Gholston.

Hackathon BCI Data analysis

NANS abstract on Injectrode electrochemistry

Co-authored a 2023 NANS abstract studying the mechanisms of increased charge injection capacity in a porous injectable electrode, using EIS, cyclic voltammetry, and voltage transient analysis to compare geometric and electrochemical surface area effects.

NANS Electrochemistry Injectrode

Neurosurgery Day presentation on PS-OCT-guided PTNS

Presented work on minimally invasive nerve layer identification using PS-OCT for optimized electrode placement in PTNS, with porcine sciatic nerve experiments showing polarization-based changes that aligned with tissue-layer transitions.

PS-OCT Porcine model Presentation

AccuGyd commercialization planning

Contributed to commercialization-facing work around AccuGyd, an image-guided neurosurgical guidance platform designed to improve workflow, reduce procedure time, support FDA 510(k) planning, and fit MRI/CT-guided minimally invasive procedures.
