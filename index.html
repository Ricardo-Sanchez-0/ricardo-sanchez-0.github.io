About

Engineering neuromodulation systems that survive contact with reality.

I study how stimulation systems behave when anatomy, device constraints, electrochemistry, and clinical workflow stop being abstract assumptions and start becoming design limits. My work sits between neural engineering, translational device development, and practical experimentation.
